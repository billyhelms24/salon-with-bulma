Ronee

/ Director + Owner /

Ronee brings over 25 years of experience as a stylist to her salon and is an expert in all facets of the salon experience. As the owner and concept designer of Free to Be Me Salon, Ronee's vision plays a vital role in making your salon experience fantastic. Ronee is dedicated to continuing education for herself and her staff of designers, helping to keep her styles new and exciting. Free to Be Me Salon intertwines Ronee's ministry and career goals, creating a unique salon experience for both her clients and staff. It is her desire that you leave your appointment encouraged and refreshed.

Kailee

/ Director /

Kailee joined Free to Be Me Salon in May, 2011. She possesses an artistic flair and fresh approach that is evident in her finished styles. Hand in hand with Ronee, she keeps the salon ideas fresh and up to date with current trends. She plays a strong role in development and future planning of Free To Be Me Salon. Kailee understands the value of each client and works to deliver a unique and relaxing appointment.

Brittny

/ Director /

Brittny offers 15 years of experience in the salon industry, including owning her own business, and becoming a certified Lanza technician in 2012. Among her many talents she excels in advanced cutting and color techniques. Brittny has worked with Matrix, Schwartzkpof, All-Nutrient, Artego, and Lanza color line. Whether her clients are looking to update their style or keep up with their current look, she uses cutting edge techniques to create a unique look for each individual client. Her fun loving personality makes communicating with clients easy and enjoyable. Brittny strives to provide her clients with a relaxed and enjoyable experience.

Malory

/ Advanaced Designer /

Malory joined Free to Be Me Salon in April 2013. She strives to understand her clients' concerns and incorporate solutions into her designs. Her experiences in the industry have sparked a love for designing men's styles. She has worked with many color lines including Schwartzkopf, Wella, Matrix, All- Nutrient and our Lanza line. Malory's greatest desire is to personalize a unique experience for each of her clients leaving them feeling refreshed and relaxed after each visit.

Brandi

/ Advanaced Designer /

Brandi is a passionate hair artist who began her career in Leipsic, Ohio more than 10 years ago. Working with the Bing Cancer Center (Riverside Hospital) she was able to help assist patients with their need for a wig type, their wig fittings and prepping. As a solo artist, she worked with the Lanza professional color line and Brazilian Blowout Professional line. Brandi's decision to join our salon is to bring her passion and skill sets to a team that will support her and her family life and provide her with the most amazing community of clients. Her warm smile, genuine love for this industry and people provide her clients with a rewarding, relaxing and professional experience.

Zoey

/ Administrative Lead /

Zoey plays a vital role in our salon as our Administrative Leader. She is often seen throughout the salon as our receptionist, making her smile the first one you see at the beginning of your service. Zoey works to bridge the gap between the stylist and the client every step of the way to ensure a smooth and relaxing service for both the stylist and client. She is also a licensed Makeup Artist and her favorite look is a full coverage base, a strong brow, and full eyelashes.

Grace

/ Designer /

Grace's exceptional skills and easy going nature makes each client feel welcomed and relaxed during their services. Joining our team in 2020 Grace brings an eagerness to learn and further grow her skill sets and develop her relationships here at Free To Be Me Salon.
