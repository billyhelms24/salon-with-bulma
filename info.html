OUR MISSION

Free To Be Me Salon was founded to celebrate God-given individuality. Each and every client is unique and our staff aspires to tailor an experience as unique as the individual receiving it. Every aspect of Free To Be Me was designed with our guests experience as the top priority. Our expertly trained stylists aspire to make every guest feel appreciated, welcomed, and confident with every service.

OUR STORY

We are a full service hair salon located in Dublin, Ohio. Our stylists were hand selected and trained by our owner to provide the best services possible and give you a professional and relaxing experience. We have a full time client relations staff to help make your experience as our guest as smooth and simple as possible. The staff within our salon are trained to tailor services to your individual needs and provide you with the best techniques, products, and education. With our unique salon philosophies, our staff works as a team getting to know all of our guests when they come in for their visit. We know how busy life can become and we made it our goal for clients to feel comfortable and confident seeing all of our stylists. With our busy clients in mind we offer online booking to help you schedule at your convenience from wherever you are.

OUR HISTORY

Originally opening in 2006 in Hilliard, Ohio as a two chair salon Free To Be Me salon has grown quite a bit. Our current location opened in 2010 in Dublin, Ohio with 6 styling chairs, two makeup chairs, and a traveling bridal service team. Free To Be Me continues to grow and reach more of the community day by day and we hope to make the most positive difference possible. Our salon was founded on the idea of fellowship and community and that is still our core principles that guide us as we grow.
